隐私政策

适用于： 《焊工证题库2026》及其相关买断版应用（包括手机桌面显示的“极简电工”等）
核心承诺： 我们极度尊重您的隐私。本应用不收集、存储或分享您的任何能识别个人身份的数据。您的刷题记录、错题本等核心数据仅存储在您的设备本地。

1. 我们收集什么数据

简短回答：我们不收集您的个人身份信息。
本应用的核心刷题功能完全离线运行，不需要互联网连接即可使用。因此：
❌ 我们不收集您的姓名、邮箱、电话号码
❌ 我们不收集您的精确地理位置信息
❌ 我们不收集您的设备识别码用于广告追踪
❌ 我们绝不使用任何第三方广告 SDK

2. 数据如何存储

您在应用中产生的所有数据（如答题记录、错题本、模拟考成绩）都仅保存在您的设备本地：
📱 使用 Apple 原生的安全沙盒机制存储在您的设备中
🔐 其他任何应用程序或网络服务都无法访问这些数据
🗑️ 当您卸载本 App 时，所有相关记录会被系统自动且永久删除

3. 数据会传输到服务器吗

我们的核心业务没有自己的服务器，所有刷题逻辑均在您的设备上本地运行：
✅ 无需注册任何账号，无需强制登录
✅ 刷题数据不会上传到任何云端
注：如果您开启了设备的 iCloud 云备份功能，您的本地应用数据可能会被 Apple 备份到您的 iCloud 账户中，这受 Apple 官方隐私政策的保护。

4. 第三方服务（内购与订阅管理）

为了给您提供“无广告、一次买断”的纯净体验，我们需要处理应用内购买（IAP）。为此，我们集成了以下必要且安全的第三方服务：
✅ RevenueCat：我们使用 RevenueCat SDK 来验证和管理您的“买断高级版”购买状态。RevenueCat 仅处理由 Apple 提供的匿名购买收据（Receipt Data），不收集、也不关联您的任何真实身份信息（如姓名或 Apple ID 账号）。

除此之外，我们不集成任何第三方服务：
❌ 无广告网络（如穿山甲、广点通）
❌ 无侵入式用户行为分析 SDK（如友盟）
❌ 无社交媒体分享 SDK

5. 设备权限

我们坚守“极简工具”的底线，只做刷题，绝不越界。本应用不需要以下任何敏感权限：
🚫 不需要相机或麦克风权限
🚫 不需要相册访问权限
🚫 不需要地理位置权限
🚫 不需要本地网络扫描权限

6. 儿童隐私

Privacy Policy

Applies to: 2026 Hangong Exam Question Bank and its related one-time purchase apps (including “Minimalist Hangong” visible on your iPhone home screen).

Core Commitment: We highly respect your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any data that can personally identify you. Your core data, such as question practice records and error logs, are stored exclusively on your device locally.

1. What Data We Collect
Short Answer: We do not collect your personally identifiable information (PII).
The app’s core question practice functionality runs entirely offline and requires no internet connection to use. Therefore:
❌ We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.
❌ We do not collect your precise location data.
❌ We do not collect device identifiers for ad tracking.
❌ We never use any third-party advertising SDKs.

2. How Data is Stored
All data generated within the app (e.g., practice records, error logs, mock exam scores) is stored locally on your device:
📱 Stored within your device using Apple’s native sandboxing mechanism.
🔐 Inaccessible to any other apps or network services.
🗑️ When you uninstall the app, all related records are automatically and permanently deleted by the system.

3. Does Data Get Transmitted to Servers?
Our core service has no servers; all question practice logic runs locally on your device:
✅ No account registration or forced login required.
✅ Practice data is never uploaded to any cloud service.
Note: If you enable iCloud backup on your device, your local app data may be backed up to your iCloud account. This is governed by Apple’s official Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services (In-App Purchases & Subscription Management)
To provide you with a clean, “ad-free, one-time purchase” experience, we process In-App Purchases (IAP). To do this, we integrate the following necessary and secure third-party service:
✅ RevenueCat: We use the RevenueCat SDK to verify and manage your “One-Time Purchase – Premium Edition” status. RevenueCat only processes anonymous purchase receipts provided by Apple. It does not collect or associate your real identity information (e.g., name or Apple ID).

Other than this, we do not integrate any third-party services:
❌ No ad networks (e.g., ChuanShanJia, GuangDianTong)
❌ No intrusive user behavior analytics SDKs (e.g., Umeng)
❌ No social media sharing SDKs

5. Device Permissions
We adhere to a “minimalist tool” principle and focus solely on question practice without overstepping boundaries. This app does not require any of the following sensitive permissions:
🚫 Camera or microphone access
🚫 Photo library access
🚫 Location services
🚫 Local Network permission

6. Children’s Privacy
This app is primarily designed for adults preparing for special operations electrician licenses. We do not intentionally collect personal data from children under 13. In fact, we do not collect personally identifiable information from any users.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time to comply with the latest Apple App Store review guidelines:
📅 Updated policies will be published on the App Store page.
✅ Your continued use of the app constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
