### FAQ

🔹 **How do I use the App?**
Select the welder exam category (熔化焊接与热切割作业), start practicing questions, review your error log, and take mock exams. All core functionality works completely offline without requiring an internet connection.

🔹 **What exam does this app cover?**
This app covers the **Welder - Fusion Welding and Thermal Cutting Operations** (焊工-熔化焊接与热切割作业) special operations certification exam, including gas welding, arc welding, gas shielded welding, and welding safety.

🔹 **What is the exam format?**
The mock exam consists of 100 questions: 70 true/false (判断题) and 30 single-choice (单选题), with a time limit of 120 minutes. A score of 80 or above is required to pass.

🔹 **Is my data stored?**
No. We respect your privacy. All practice records, wrong-answer logs, and settings remain strictly on your local device. No cloud upload or account registration is required.
